# Backlink Outreach Email Templates

General notes:
- Always personalize the [bracketed] fields — generic mail-merge blasts get ignored or marked spam.
- Keep it short. 3-5 sentences max.
- Lead with value to them, not a request for you.
- Send from hello@petcost-calculator.com (matches the contact page).

---
